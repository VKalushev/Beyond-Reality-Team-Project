How to Use

This tool is a prototype, controls are a best-effort made in the time available.
  • Use right click and the arrow keys to move the camera around.
  • Use the 'Place Asset' button to place down an asset. Note some assets may trigger a freeze.
  • Use the 'Delete Asset' button to delete an asset from the project.
  • Click on an asset to view its properties, and use the WASD and QE buttons to move it around.
  • Press the Escape key to deselect an asset.
